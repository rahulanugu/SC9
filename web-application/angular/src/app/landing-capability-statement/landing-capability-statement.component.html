Machine Learning | AI | Risk Management

ScriptChain Health utilizes deep learning algorithms to provide individualized risk assessment and predicted readmission risk to healthcare providers through interfacing with major EMRs.

Our actionable insights can save valuable physician time, improve workflow, and drastically reduce overhead costs through redfucing readmission rate.

As a Minority Owned Small Business, ScriptChain Health is committed to excellence and creating innovative and cost-effective solutions for our health provider clients.

Data Integration / Transfer

Through being developed with integration with the Big Five EMR systems in mind: Cerner, Athena, Allscripts, EPIC, and Meditech, we interface directly with data pulled from their APIs.

By facilitating the movement of data across hospitals and providers in a customizable patient profile, we ensure that patient data is safe and easily accessible.

This means:

  • Any Physician Using ScriptChain Health Can Access Patient Data
  • Drastically Lowered Wait Times
  • Automated Data Interpretation Between Patient Appointments
  • Cloud-Based Storage
